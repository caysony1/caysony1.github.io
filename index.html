About Me

Hello ! My name is Cayson Yim and I am currently pursuing a Master of Software Engineering student at the University of California, Irvine. I hold a bachelor's degree in Informatics, with a background specializing in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) from UCI, and through my studies, I've come to realize, learn, and understand that there is a responsibility to ensure universal access to information, be it digitally or not - i.e. Informatics. As a technologist my goal is to create positive, powerful, and lasting experiences in the digital world and beyond. My interest lie in the intersection of human experience and software development. I am committed to learning and developing innovative solutions to various kinds of user interaction challenges through using human-centered design methods and practices. Some of my favorite hobbies and interests include skiing, playing video games, watching movies, listening to music, gastronomy, and astrophotography. Thanks for visting my personal portfolio!
